Some Dog Care Tips

  • Feeding: Depending on your dog's age and size, you should feed your dog between 1 meal to 4 meals a day.
  • Exercise: Daily exercises is important for your dog's physical health. We suggest daily walks or playing catch with your dog,
  • Grooming: On a regular basis, clipping your dog's nails and brushing their fur, for example, should be done to maintain their hygeine.
  • Health Care: Your dog should get regular check-ups, get vaccines (if suggested by your veterinarian), and have daily inspections of fleas to ensure your dog's well-being.
  • Socializing: To ensure your dog is properly trained, teach them some basic commands and make sure they are used to being around other people and animals. The most important thing to remember is to continue to build a strong bond with your dog.


For more information on how to take care of your dog, we suggest that you visit ASPCA.
