Some Cat Care Tips

  • Feeding: Your cat main diet must contain an amino acid called taurine for better health of their heart and eyes. Make sure you provide fresh water to your cat on a daily basis.
  • Scratch: Cats need to scratch in order to trim their old nails. We suggest you by a scratch post or a scratch pad for them.
  • Grooming: Brush or comb your cat's fur in order to reduce shedding and hairball accumulation.
  • Health Care: Your cat should get regular check-ups (at least once a year), get vaccines (if suggested by your veterinarian) and it is suggested to keep your cat indoors.
  • Housing: Make sure to clean your cat's bed and litter box on a regular basis. Refill the litter box at least once a week and don't use any ammonia, deodorant or anything made of lemon to clean the box.
